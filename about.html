About Us

Welcome to Milestone Travels

Best Travel Agency
Since 2008

Milestone Travels is a team of Himalayan travel enthusiasts dedicated to helping you explore and understand this country better. We are committed to discovering and exploring unknown and off-the-beaten-path destinations. Having personally visited these places and stayed in the hotels, we ensure that our recommendations are based on firsthand experience.


Milestone Travels was established in 2008 by Abrar to cater to the needs of local tourists visiting India. Initially focused on serving Indian customers, we expanded in 2008 to include an inbound department to manage the influx of foreign tourists and ensure their complete satisfaction.


A strong commitment to quality is at the heart of our DNA. Milestone Travels strives to make your trip personalized and well-organized.


At Milestone Travels we offer the best possible prices on the market. We offer a wide variety of slots for you to choose your favorite from.
