Himachal Car Tour Package

DESCRIPTION

  • 05 Days / 04 Nights
  • Age 12+
  • Delhi-Shimla-Kullu-Manali

Itinerary

Day 1 - Delhi -Shimla
  • On arrival at Delhi Our transporter will meet you and transfer by to Shimla . Arrive Shimla check in hotel . Evening free to visit Lakkar Bazar, Shimla Mall Road . Dinner and overnight stay at Hotel.
Day 2 - Shimla (Full day trip to Kufri )
  • After Breakfast proceed for Full day sightseeing to Jakhoo Hills , Green Valley , White Flower, Kufri, Indira Tourist Park & Mini zoo. Evening Free to visit to Mall Road. Overnight stay & Dinner at Hotel.
Day 3 - Shimla -Kullu - Manali
  • After Breakfast Drive from Shimla to Manali. Enroute visit Kullu. Evening Check in hotel. Free to visit Mall Road. Dinner & Overnight stay at hotel.
Day 4 - Manali (Half Day Local Sightseeing and Rohtang Pass/Solang Valley)
  • After Breakfast Visit Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, Tibetan Monastery and Vashisht Village. And excursion visiting Kothi Gorge, Gulaba, Marhi, Rohtang Pass and Solang Valley .Evening free to visit Mall Road .Back to Hotel . Dinner & Overnight stay at hotel. (Note: Rohtang pass not included it will be your own)
Day 5 - Manali - Delhi Drop
  • Morning after Breakfast Check out from hotel and proceed to Delhi. Drop at Delhi airport, railway station with onward journey.
Note :- Rohtang Pass / Snow Point Sightseeing will be possible if Govt. can Allow our transport for the Sightseeing. Otherwise client have to take another transport for Rohtang Pass / Snow Point Sightseeing as per clients own cost.

Inclusion / Exclusion

  • 2 Night accommodation in Manali
  • 2 Nights accommodation in Shimla
  • Welcome drinks on arrival
  • Accommodation in Budget hotel and standard Room
  • Transfer from Railway Station/Airport to Hotel
  • Transport for 4 nights & 5 days
  • City to city transfer and sightseeing as per the itinerary
