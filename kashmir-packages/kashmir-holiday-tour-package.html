Kashmir Holiday Tour Package

DESCRIPTION

  • 09 Days / 08 Nights
  • Age 12+
  • Srinagar-Sonmarg-Gulmarg-Srinagar

Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival Jammu
  • Arrive at the Jammu Airport and our representative will welcome you the airport and transfer to the Katra hotel. Overnight stay in Katra.
Day 2 - Katra – Patnitop
  • Morning After Breakfast checkout the hotel and proceed to the Patnitop check in the hotel. After that proceed to the local sightseeing of Patnitop visit places like Billo ki powri,PatniTop Circular Road, Baglihar Dam, Sudh Mahadev,Naag Mandir,Sanasar LakeNathatop.Dinner and overnight stay in Hotel.
Day 3 - Jammu – Srinagar
  • Morning after breakfast checkout the hotel and drive to Srinagar chek in to the house boat. Dinner and overnight stay in house boat.
Day 4 - Srinagar local Sightseeing
  • Morning with heavy breakfast check out the house boat and chek in into the Srinagar hotel. And proceed to the local sightseeing of Srinagar visit the places like chasma sahi bagh, Mughal garden, Dal Lake, famous sankracharya temple, etc. Overnight stay in Deluxe house boat.
Day 5 - Srinagar – Sonmarg
  • After taking breakfast Proceed to Sonmarg (Meadow of Gold).The distance of 83 kms will be covered in about 2-3 hours. Sonmarg lies in Sindh Valley streamed with flowers and surrounded by mountains. Sonmarg is also the base for some interesting treks to the high altitude Himalayan Lakes. Thajiwas, Overnight stay at Houseboat
Day 6 - Srinagar- Gulmarg
  • Morning leave for Gulmarg full day excursion (Meadow of Flowers) 2730 Mts. Above the sea level. Gulmarg has one of the best ski slopes in the world and highest golf course with 18 holes. If weather permits one can also have the view of Nanga Parbat, the naked mountain that is over 26,000 feet’s and dominates the entire region, also enjoy cable car (Gondola) for mountain ride up to khilanmarg. In the evening back to Srinagar and overnight stay at Gulmarg hotel.
Day 7 - Gulmarg
  • Morning after breakfast full day free to explore the snow in Gulmarg. Full day leisure.Evening dinner and overnight stay at hotel.
Day 8 - Gulmarg – Pahalgam
  • Today you will be drive to Pahalgam (the valley of shepherds). The total distance is about 85 kms, Will be covered in about 3 hrs. Drive through the pine forests. And enjoy Lidder stream. Later walk along pony trails with a magnificent view of the mountains, and can go to Chandanwadi, village ardu and others (Sightseeing by Horse Ride) evening return and overnight stay at Pahalgam at hotel.
Day 9 - Departure at Jammu/Katra
  • Morning after breakfast check out the hotel and after will be transfer the Katra Railway station for your onward journey. The tour end with the sweet memories and our services.
