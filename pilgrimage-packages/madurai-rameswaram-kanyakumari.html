Madurai Rameswaram Kanyakumari Package

DESCRIPTION

  • 05 Days / 04 Nights
  • Age 12+
  • Madurai-Rameswaram-Kanyakumari–Cochin

Itinerary

Day 01 : Cochin Airport – Madurai
  • Today Cochin Airport / station Pickup to Direct to Madurai. Overnight stay at hotel in Madurai.
Day 02 : Madurai Sightseeing – Rameswaram
  • Today Morning Dharsan to Madurai Meenakshi Amman temple, Alagar temple, and Thirumalai Nayak palace, and after the visit Proceed to Rameswaram Hotel Transfer. Night Stay at Rameswaram.
Day 03 : Rameswaram Sightseeing
  • Today Morning Bath to Sea ( Kadal ) and After Sea Bath Go to 22 Theetham Both to Ramanatha Swami temple Inside and after both dharsan to Ramanatha Swami temple and after visit drive to Kanyakumari.
Day 04 : Kanyakumari Sightseeing – Cochin
  • Today Morning 6.30 AM Visit to Sunrise, Mukkadal Sangamam and after Breakfast visit to Vivekananda Rock Memorial, Thiruvalluvar statue, Kanyakuamri Beach. In evening drive to Cochin. Night stay at Cochin.
Day 05: Cochin Departure
  • Today Early Morning drop at Cochin Airport with onward your Destination.

Package Inclusions

  • All taxes included
