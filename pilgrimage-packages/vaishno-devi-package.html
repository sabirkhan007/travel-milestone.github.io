Vaishno Devi Package

DESCRIPTION

  • 03 Days / 02 Nights
  • Age 12+
  • Delhi-Katra-Delhi

Itinerary

Day 1 : Delhi to Katra :
  • Start from Delhi in the night & proceed to Katra by Volvo A/C Bus .Overnight at Bus.
Day 2 : Katra
  • Pickup from Bus stand & Transfers to Hotel. Checkin Hotel & After refreshment Trek to Mata Vaishno Devi Darshan . Late Night Back to Hotel & Overnight at Hotel.
Day 3 : Katra - Delhi
  • After Breakfast Checkout Hotel & visit local Katra Market . Catch the Bus in the evening . Overnight at Bus .Back to Delhi next day morning.

Package Inclusions

  • Volvo Tickets from Delhi – Katra – Delhi.
  • 01 Night stay at Katra Hotel..
  • 01 Breakfasts & 01 Dinners at hotel.
  • All applicable hotel taxes.

Package Excludes

  • Meals not specified above.
  • Surcharges applicable.
  • Helicopter Tickets is not included.
  • Travel and Medical Insurance.
  • GST of 4.50%.
  • Tips and Porterage.
  • Expenses of personal nature.
